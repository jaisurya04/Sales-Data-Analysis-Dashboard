# Sales-Data-Analysis-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed for regional sales analysis. Tracks Year-over-Year (YoY) performance metrics, Regional Manager performance against category targets, and highlights operational risks like delivery delays and product return rates for data-driven business decisions.
# B2B Regional Sales Performance & Risk Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on transitioning a regional sales company from a fragmented, manual spreadsheet reporting system into an automated, interactive Power BI decision-making solution. Designed specifically for non-technical sales leadership, this dashboard provides a complete business story to monitor executive KPIs, identify territory revenue leakage, and mitigate operational risks during monthly business reviews.

## 📊 Key Features & Analysis Angles
- **Overall Business Health:** Executive-level overview tracking sales growth, profit margins, and cumulative order counts.
- **Year-over-Year (YoY) Metrics:** Detailed calculations showing percentage growth or decline across sales, profits, returns, and logistics delays.
- **Manager & Target Optimization:** Dynamic data mapping matrix that tracks individual Regional Managers against localized category-specific sales targets to isolate gaps.
- **Customer Experience & Risk Tracking:** Integration of supply chain metrics analyzing product return rates and shipping delays to spotlight operational bottlenecks and quality issues.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
- **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
- **Data Transformation:** Power Query (ETL, Data Cleaning, and Structuring)
- **Data Modeling:** Star Schema / Multi-dimensional Relationship Modeling
- **Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for Time-Intelligence functions, YoY % changes, and conditional formatting rules.
- **Design Philosophy:** Executive Dashboard Design, Strategic Business Storytelling for Non-Technical Stakeholders.

## 📈 Key Insights Derived
1. Identified specific regional categories where sales volumes were increasing but profitability was declining due to high product returns.
2. Isolated underperforming product lines managed by regional teams that consistently missed target thresholds despite high overall territory revenue.
3. Correlated shipping methods and logistics delays directly with increased customer complaints and localized return spikes.
